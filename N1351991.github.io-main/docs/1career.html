Career Aspirations 

Computer Games Developer

Introduction

I aspire to be a Computer Games Developer. To work with advanced tech, creating innovative and creative game experiences. This work balances out my passion for coding and problem-solving while also adhering to my artistic roots. I am mostly interested in working in a team setting, preferably international, that optimises hybrid and remote work. I prefer my job to be flexible and enjoyable, with lots of time to spend on my outside life.

Research and Requirements

Based on research, the primary skills and knowledge needed in pursuit of this role are:

  • Good knowledge of programming languages C++, C# or Python
  • Ability to work on game engines such as Unity and Unreal Engine
  • Understanding of object-oriented programming
  • Good collaboration and communication skills
  • Visual and innovative
  • Strong problem solving skills

My lectures emphasise the need for emotional intelligence, adaptability, and teamwork in the workplace. Therefore, I continuously develop my technical and interpersonal skills through coursework, personal projects and extracurriculars.
